About

Hello there everybody and welcome to our website called Scripturient.It offers you an incredible chance to publish and write your own stories upon want you wish. Even better is the fact that Scripturient publishes more than 2000 stories per week for you to read,learnfrom and enjoy. In addition to, this is at no cost,you will only have to make an account. Did you know that even better, famous and amazing writers and editors publish their views on creative writing.Pretty amazing, don't you think? Scripturient encourages and lets you to develop your writing skills within a safe and positive environment. The idea of anonymous writers posting their stories encourages you tofocus on the work instead of the people who wrote it and etc.
